What is DevSight?

About Me

The ICT Seminar entitled DevSight is an event that invites professional developers to share stories and insights with students. This event aims to motivate students (ICT class) in undergoing learning. In addition, this event is expected to be able to broaden students' insights about ICT in the professional world.
